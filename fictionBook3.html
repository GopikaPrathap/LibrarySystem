To Kill A Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Genre: Fiction

To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee is a profound exploration of racial injustice and moral growth. Set in the fictional town of Maycomb, Alabama during the 1930s, the novel follows the awakening of a young girl, Jean Louise ("Scout") Finch, to racism and prejudice in her town. The story portrays the young narrator's passage from innocence to experience as her father confronts the racist justice system of the rural, Depression-era South.
