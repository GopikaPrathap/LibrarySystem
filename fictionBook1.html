The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Genre: Fiction

The Great Gatsby is a 1925 novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald. It is set in Jazz Age New York and tells the tragic story of Jay Gatsby, a self-made millionaire, and his pursuit of Daisy Buchanan, a wealthy young woman whom he loved in his youth. The novel is narrated by Nick Carraway, a Yale graduate who becomes a bond salesman and moves to a Long Island suburb called West Egg. The novel depicts Carraway's interactions with Gatsby and Gatsby's obsession to reunite with Daisy.
