Gone Girl

Author: Gillian Flynn

Genre: Mystery

‘Gone Girl’ is a suspenseful psychological thriller written by Gillian Flynn. The story revolves around Nick and Amy Dunne, a seemingly happy couple whose marriage is thrown into turmoil when Amy goes missing on their fifth wedding anniversary. Nick comes under investigation as the prime suspect in her disappearance. The novel was published in 2012 and was popular, making the New York Times Best Seller list.
